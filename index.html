Our Model Get in Touch
What we do

Three pillars. One repeatable model.

We have forged a strong triangle of impact promoting education, innovation, and entrepreneurial ecosystems. As a result, we generate high-volume, high-quality medical device startups. BMTT is the first-ever feeding studio implemented in this way.

🎓

Education

Training undergraduate, master's, and healthcare students to be career-ready by instilling business modeling, customer discovery methodology, and real-world networking know-how.

💡

Innovation

Discovering clinical needs through shadowing and structured discovery, analyzing markets rigorously, and engineering customer-validated solutions with student-powered teams.

🔗

Ecosystem

Growing local ecosystems one startup at a time by creating a more capable workforce, building select ventures with local talent, and expanding into new college towns with lasting impact.

The Difference

We operate upstream.

Most accelerators and studios wait for founders to arrive with ideas already formed. BMTT runs students through full-cycle programming that begins well before any concept exists: clinical shadowing, customer discovery, unmet need identification, market analysis, and validated venture design. By the time a team completes our curriculum, they are not pitching early-stage concepts. They are presenting de-risked venture frameworks with documented demand, defined user populations, and credible paths to market.
