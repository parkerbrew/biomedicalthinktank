Feeding Studio · Medtech · Student Innovation

Turning untapped academic innovation into real-world healthcare impact.

Thousands of student innovations are collecting dust on shelves. Not because students lack the ability to solve real clinical problems, but because there is no mechanism in place to systematically scale that ability and translate it into real companies. BMTT is that mechanism: a venture formation platform that builds the infrastructure students need to identify unmet clinical needs, validate solutions, and see those solutions through to market.

Our Model Get in Touch
The Engine

A startup engine built around the problems that actually matter.

BMTT embeds students directly alongside practicing clinicians and healthcare providers, creating the conditions for authentic, high-fidelity problem identification. From there, students move through our proprietary AI-enabled curriculum: an iterative process of problem scoping and clarification, structured customer discovery, solution ideation, and additional validation rounds designed to build conviction in the right ideas and eliminate the wrong ones early.

🏥

Clinical Access

Students work directly alongside practicing clinicians and healthcare providers, gaining firsthand exposure to the unmet needs and systemic friction points that never make it into a lecture hall.

🤖

AI-Enabled Curriculum

Our proprietary curriculum guides students through iterative problem scoping, customer discovery, solution ideation, and further validation, compressing years of hard-won startup learning into a single program.

📐

De-risked Output

Every stage is engineered to build conviction in the right ideas and eliminate the wrong ones early. The output is not a pitch deck. It is a venture framework with documented clinical demand, a defined user population, and a credible path to market.

The Difference

We operate upstream.

Most accelerators and studios wait for founders to arrive with ideas already formed. BMTT runs students through full-cycle programming that begins well before any concept exists: clinical shadowing, customer discovery, unmet need identification, market analysis, and validated venture design. By the time a team completes our curriculum, they are not pitching early-stage concepts. They are presenting de-risked venture frameworks with documented demand, defined user populations, and credible paths to market.
